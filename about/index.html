About me

Posted on Mar 9, 2020

Hi, there! Using these pages to document the loot of my adventures and expeditions. What they will be, that is too early to say.

10 things I love

  1. My kids to bits and Pia, their mother, because it is the only thing that matters
  2. Data and statistics because of the beauty it beholds
  3. Keyboard mastery and Emacs training as they represent the forgotten art of powerful steep learning curve UX. I think
  4. Slide show presentations because they have been misunderstood and abused for too long
  5. Jokes, much like unexpected novel ideas, art or philosophy. More or less the same thing
  6. Nonfiction and fiction in particular as it is a factual description of our inner reality
  7. Lisp programming because of its minimal aestethics and because it is lovely to type
  8. 8-bit computers, as that is my age
  9. Annoying music as non-annoying music drives me mad
  10. Borges, as also he was a friend of recursion
(->>
 ((fn fib [a b]
    (lazy-seq
     (cons a (fib b (+ a b))))) 0 1)
 (take 20))

10 more things I enjoy:

  1. Emotions, they are 70% of what we actually mean
  2. Multiple perspectives, otherwise all would be flat
  3. Journalism, as it is can, in fact, also be used for good
  4. Systems, be they techical, ecological, psycological or artistic, they are as attractive as they are brittle
  5. Maps in the widest sense of the word
  6. Looking people in the eyes regularily as it is the only way we can feel what they feel
  7. Randomness, because it helps us discover interesting patterns
  8. Hacking groups of people because when it works it can bring so much joy
  9. Youtube, for it offers all the lectures we will ever need
  10. Reading and writing lists, as lists, one suspect, are the true reason we made the leap and started to read and write; to remember, not to express

I am Sami Rafael Kallinen, nice to meet you.
